# The Texas-Israel Tech Corridor: A Strategic Shift

Historically, the 'default' path for an Israeli startup was Tel Aviv to San Francisco. But in 2026, the data shows a massive pivot toward Texas—specifically Austin and the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

### Why Texas?
For a Seed or Series A founder, Texas offers a 'Triple Threat' of advantages that California no longer guarantees:
1. **Capital Efficiency:** Lower operational overhead means your runway extends by 30-40%.
2. **Strategic EDOs:** Organizations like the Texas Economic Development Corporation are rolling out the red carpet for Israeli cybersecurity and ag-tech firms.
3. **The Talent Moat:** With the migration of major enterprise players, the talent pool in Texas is now as deep as it is affordable.
